LEAVE POLICY

Annual Leave:
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave must be requested at least 7 days in advance through the HR portal.
Unused leave can be carried forward to the next year, up to a maximum of 10 days.

Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.
Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year.

Maternity Leave:
Female employees are entitled to 90 days of paid maternity leave.
Maternity leave can begin 30 days before the expected delivery date.

---

REMOTE WORK POLICY

Eligibility:
Employees who have completed 6 months of service are eligible to apply for remote work.
Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week.
All remote work requests must be approved by the direct manager.

Equipment:
The company will provide a laptop and necessary software for remote work.
Employees are responsible for their own internet connection.
Any equipment damage must be reported within 24 hours.

---

SALARY AND BENEFITS

Salary Review:
Salary reviews are conducted every 12 months in January.
Performance ratings directly impact salary increment percentages.
The average increment for a good performance rating is 10-15%.

Health Insurance:
All employees receive full medical insurance coverage from day one.
Coverage includes the employee, spouse, and up to 3 children.
Dental and vision coverage is included in the plan.

---

CODE OF CONDUCT

Workplace Behavior:
All employees must maintain a respectful and professional environment.
Harassment of any kind will result in immediate termination.
Employees must protect company confidential information at all times.

Conflict of Interest:
Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest to HR.
Working for a competitor while employed at TechCorp is strictly prohibited.

---

RESIGNATION POLICY

Notice Period:
Employees must provide a minimum of 30 days written notice before resignation.
Senior roles require a 60-day notice period.
The company reserves the right to waive the notice period.

Final Settlement:
Final salary and dues will be processed within 7 working days after the last day.
All company equipment must be returned on or before the last working day.
